About EMICalc India

Built for Indian borrowers who deserve clarity before signing a loan.

Why we built this

Most loan calculators in India show only the basic EMI. They hide what matters — the total interest you'll pay over 20 years, the processing fee, the GST on that fee, and how much you'd save by prepaying even a small amount.

EMICalc India was built to fix that. We show you the true cost of any loan, let you compare all major banks side by side, and give you tools to save money — before you sign anything.

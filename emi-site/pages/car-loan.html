How much down payment is needed for a car loan?

Banks typically finance 80–90% of on-road price. You need 10–20% as down payment. A higher down payment reduces EMI and total interest significantly.

Is a car loan better from a bank or car dealership?

Bank rates are usually 0.5–1% lower than dealer-arranged financing. Always get a bank quote first, then use it to negotiate with the dealer's finance arm.

Can I get a car loan for a used car?

Yes. Most banks finance used cars up to 5 years old at rates 1–3% higher than new car loans. Loan amount is capped at 80% of the car's current market value.

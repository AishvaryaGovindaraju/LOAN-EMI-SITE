Education Loan Guide

SBI offers the lowest education loan rates in India starting at 8.15% p.a. under the SBI Student Loan Scheme. Up to ₹7.5 lakh: no collateral needed. Above ₹7.5 lakh: collateral required. Interest during the moratorium period is typically added to the principal.

What is the moratorium period in education loans?

Moratorium = course duration + 6 months (or 1 year after getting a job, whichever is earlier). No EMI during this period, but interest accrues and is either paid monthly or added to principal.

Is education loan interest tax deductible?

Yes. Under Section 80E, the entire interest paid on education loans is deductible from taxable income for up to 8 consecutive years. This applies to the borrower (student or parent).

What is the maximum education loan amount in India?

SBI and PSU banks: up to ₹7.5L without collateral, up to ₹1.5 Cr with collateral for top institutions. Private banks like HDFC Credila and Avanse offer up to ₹75L–₹1 Cr for overseas education.

Can I get an education loan without a co-applicant?

Most banks require a parent or guardian as co-applicant. Some private lenders and NBFCs offer loans to working professionals without a co-applicant for executive education programs.

Bank Rate FAQs

Which bank has the lowest home loan rate in India?

SBI and PNB typically offer the lowest home loan rates starting at 8.50% p.a. for salaried applicants with CIBIL score above 750. Rates change periodically based on RBI repo rate.

Do all banks use the same EMI formula?

Yes. All Indian banks use the reducing balance method: EMI = [P×R×(1+R)^N]/[(1+R)^N–1]. The only difference is the interest rate each bank charges.

Can I negotiate the interest rate with a bank?

Yes — especially if you have a CIBIL score above 750, a long relationship with the bank, or a high salary. It's always worth asking for a 0.25–0.5% reduction.

Are the rates shown here live/real-time?

These are representative rates as of 2025 for salaried applicants with good credit scores. Actual rates may vary based on your profile, loan amount, and the bank's current offers. Always confirm with the bank before deciding.

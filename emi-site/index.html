Frequently Asked Questions

What is the EMI formula?

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1] where P = Principal, R = Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100), N = Tenure in months.

Which bank has the lowest home loan rate in India 2025?

SBI offers home loans starting at 8.50% p.a. for salaried applicants with good CIBIL scores (750+). Use our Bank Comparison tool for current rates.

What is a safe EMI-to-salary ratio?

Your total EMIs should not exceed 40–50% of monthly take-home salary. Most banks reject applications above this threshold.

Does prepayment really save money?

Yes. Paying one extra EMI per year on a 20-year home loan can save 2–4 years of tenure and lakhs in interest. Use our Prepayment Calculator.

What hidden charges should I check?

Processing fee (0.5–2%), GST on processing fee (18%), prepayment penalty (2–4%), late payment charges (2%/month), CIBIL report fee.

Is this calculator accurate for SBI/HDFC/ICICI loans?

Yes. All banks use the same standard reducing balance EMI formula. The only variable is the interest rate.
